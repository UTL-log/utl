About Universal Trans Logistics

Universal Trans Logistics is a premium dispatch service provider specializing in load management, freight matching, and carrier support. We connect owner operators and fleets with the highest paying loads while handling all paperwork, negotiation, and broker coordination.

Our expert team works 24/7 to ensure you stay on the road with maximum profitability and minimum hassle. From rate negotiation to route optimization and detention claims, we handle every detail so you can focus on driving.

We don't just move freight — we move your business forward with precision, transparency, and relentless dedication to your success.

Our Vision

To become a leading logistics and dispatch company by building long-term partnerships with carriers, leveraging smart strategies and cutting-edge technology. We envision a future where every owner operator has access to premium freight, transparent communication, and consistent growth opportunities.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, transparent, and efficient dispatch services that maximize profits for our clients. We are committed to providing 24/7 support, aggressive rate negotiation, and seamless operations so our drivers can focus on what they do best — driving.
